what we do

Does your level of visibility match your level of accomplishment?

Have you done more than your reputation gives you credit for?

Perhaps you're beginning a new career chapter and trying to get noticed for a breakthrough opportunity?

We help bring visibility to your capabilities. For anyone who has ever thought, “Yeah, I should probably post something” or “I should put up more content” we help make that goal reachable.

We don't tell you what to say or invent a new version of the professional you. Instead, we created a program that will help you figure out what you want to say and how you want to say it on your own terms.

We ask thought provoking questions and provide some structure and accountability.

Program Schedule

Visible Work is a cohort based 10-week program where you'll get the following:

  • Consulting on your professional narrative.
  • An accountability partner
  • A step-by-step schedule of goals and milestones for sharing your narrative.
  • Informative guides on different social media channels and strategies.
